5 Data Security Measures

Enterprise-Grade Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Encryption

All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption. Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard algorithms.

Access Controls

Role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access customer data on a need-to-know basis.

Regular Audits

Regular security audits, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessments ensure our defenses remain strong.

Incident Response

Comprehensive incident response plan with immediate notification procedures for any potential data breaches.

Staff Training

Regular training programs ensure all staff understand data protection obligations and security best practices.

Monitoring

24/7 monitoring systems detect and alert us to any suspicious activity or unauthorized access attempts.

Your Role in Data Security

While we implement strong security measures, you can help protect your information by:

  • • Using strong, unique passwords
  • • Keeping login credentials confidential
  • • Logging out from shared devices
  • • Reporting suspicious activities
  • • Keeping contact information updated
  • • Being cautious with public Wi-Fi

7 Your Rights Under NDPR

You Have Control Over Your Data

The Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) gives you specific rights regarding your personal information. We respect and facilitate these rights.

Right of Access

Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you, including how it's being used.

Response time: 30 days

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you.

Response time: 15 days

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data when there's no legitimate reason for continued processing.

Response time: 30 days

Right to Data Portability

Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format for transfer to another service.

Response time: 30 days

Right to Object

Object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing or other legitimate interests.

Response time: Immediate for marketing

Right to Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your personal data while we address concerns about accuracy or lawfulness.

Response time: 15 days

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time where consent is the legal basis.

Response time: Immediate

Right to Lodge a Complaint

File a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission if you believe your rights have been violated.

Contact: NDPC directly

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer using the contact information provided at the end of this policy. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Submit written request

2

Verify your identity
