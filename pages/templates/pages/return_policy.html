  • • Manufacturing defects and material faults
  • • Premature component failure under normal use
  • • Electrical performance issues
  • • Free repair or replacement of defective parts
  • • Labor costs for warranty repairs

Not Covered:

  • • Damage from misuse, abuse, or negligence
  • • Normal wear and tear
  • • Damage from power surges or electrical faults
