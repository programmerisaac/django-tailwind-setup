Our Story

Tamaktech was founded in 2008 with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide reliable, high-quality electrical solutions that power Nigeria's growth and development. What started as a small electrical equipment supply business has evolved into one of Nigeria's most trusted electrical services companies.

Our journey began when our founder, recognizing the critical need for reliable power solutions in Nigeria, decided to establish a company that would bridge the gap between quality electrical equipment and professional installation services. From our humble beginnings in Lagos, we've expanded our reach across Nigeria, serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of Nigeria's electrical revolution, pioneering sustainable energy solutions through solar installations, providing backup power through generators, and ensuring electrical safety through our professional services. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of over 5,000 customers and completion of more than 500 major projects.
